Full Names (internal) = Camel Case (eg - camelCase)
Full Names (classes, interfaces, structs, delegates) = Pascal Case (eg - PascalCase)
Player items = 'p' (eg - pItem or pHealth)
Enemy items = 'e' (eg - eItem or eHealth)
Main items = 'm' (eg - mField or mPoints)
UI elements = 'u' (uMenu - uSettings)
gameManager elements instances, etc: '_' (eg - _deck or _gamewin)
